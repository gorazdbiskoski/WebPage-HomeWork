Playing the Guitar

I have been playing the guitar since elementary school. The first and only guitar I got was for my birthday. Since then, I started having private classes with my professor who helped me immensely and helped me greatly to advance my ability to play the guitar. I am a perfectionist, so at the start, every time I messed up I would get angry and start playing the song from the beginning, I would do that every time until I got it perfectly right. My professor even made a difficult to play song for me so I would practice everyday until I got it perfect.

Nowadays I don't take up any private lessons, I just pick up my guitar now and then just to remind myself how to play and for simply clearing my mind.

Playing Video Games

I would say that playing video games is the first hobby I had picked up. I have been playing video games since I was a kid, even before kindergarten. My brother introduced me to gaming, and the first game he introduced me to was counter strike 1.6, and shortly after grand theft auto san andreas. Playing video games has always calmed me down in stressful situations. It is a space where you could meet new people across the world and connect with them, although some people find video games dangerous of kids, I believe it is all a misconception.
